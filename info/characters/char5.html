Character information.

Mr. Puzzles

Origin Story

Mr puzzles has no origin. He randomly appears in a video one day, set in the backrooms. He proceeds to wonder around aimlessly until he finds some weird base.
So he uses all the weapons he has for some reason and tries to break through, no wonder, it fails. So in a fit of rage, he just decides to shoot the camera.
However we do know how he got in the backrooms, he was playing around with one of the corrupted models and clipped into the backrooms. What a fucking mess.

Bomb incident

This guy man. This guy. Have you ever heard of a subspace tripmine? Yeah of course you did.
You know what will happen next. He gets his slimy hands on one, beats it up, and, what do you know, it explodes!
Punctured several holes in spacetime. Due to this, the backrooms collapsed and the two dimensions merged into what we call, the castle.
Endless labyrinth with weird broken physics and a huge hole of a corrupted interdimensional mass at the center.
Occasionally random objects and people from random times and dimensions come out of it.

Return

On a random day in the castle, the hole started to convulse rapidly, and sent Mr puzzles and Soviet into the castle.
The hole closed and never returned. Just wow.
